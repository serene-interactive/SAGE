## What is SAGE?

SAGE is a wellness AI agent powered by the SAGE model family — a line of small language models purpose-built for herbalism, stress management, naturopathic wellness, and lifestyle support.

Unlike generalist AI models trained on massive, uncurated datasets, SAGE was engineered specifically for health and wellness. Every response is grounded in cited research and reliable sources — not SEO-optimized content or commercial rankings.

SAGE is not a doctor and does not replace one. It provides educational and informational guidance to help you make informed wellness decisions.

## The SAGE Model Family

SAGE is powered by a family of small language models (SLMs) designed for efficiency, accuracy, and portability.

| Model | Size | Description |
|-------|------|-------------|
| **SAGE-3.0-4B** | 4B | Latest generation. Powers the SAGE agent. Optimized for factual reliability and wellness accuracy. |
| SAGE-2.5-4B | 4B | Previous generation. Stable and capable. |
| SAGE-2.2-4B | 4B | Agentic variant. Trained twice on agentic trajectories. |
| SAGE-2.0-4B | 4B | Agentic angle. Early agentic training. |
| SAGE-1.0-4B | 4B | Baseline general purpose. First generation. |
| SAGE-9B | 9B | Cascade partner with SAGE-1.0-4B in production. |
| SAGEmini-2B | 2B | Compact variant. Designed for local and mobile deployment. |
| SAGE2mini-2B | 2B | Compact upgrade with agentic capabilities. Rivals SAGE-1.0-4B. |

Small models mean SAGE can run efficiently on modest hardware — including mobile devices — making wellness guidance accessible to everyone, everywhere.

## Privacy

SAGE follows an **Exception-Based Audit** model:

- **No conversations are stored.** All interactions are processed in real-time and discarded.
- **No personal data is collected.** SAGE operates without user accounts or identifiable profiles.
- **Critical Events only.** System failures, safety triggers, and user-reported bugs are logged for diagnostics — anonymized by default.
- **You're in control.** Use `/privacy` to view, delete, or opt out of any data retention.

For full details, see [PRIVACY.md](./PRIVACY.md).
